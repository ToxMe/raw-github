Frequently asked questions

When the index is entirely marketing speak and buzzwords

How do I use this?

Select open with Phobos from the menu.

What services do you support?

Github and Bitbucket.

How much does this service cost?

It's 100% free as Cloudflare covers most of our bandwidth costs.

What about caching?

We tell Cloudflare to cache files as long as they aren't modified on the backend side, telling browsers to cache for 30 minutes.

Do you modify data at all?

For sanity reasons we have Cloudflare minify js/css/html, every other feature is disabled.

Do you block Tor users/display security warnings?

We have all of Cloudflares security options as low as possible.

Can I use this on my production site?

Sure, no promises though, because if you haven't noticed yet this is free.

What do you do on the server side?

We strip out most of the headers and come up with a sane sounding content-type, then we polish it up, stick a nice bowtie on, and hope for the best.

How much does this cost to run?

A whole $5 a month for a VPS running Nginx. Fancy, I know.

Where is the source?

Right here, this site is hosting itself.

Can I host my personal webpage on this?

I mean if you really want, I'm not going to stop you. The URL might be a bit odd though.

I plan on using this in order to DDoS the Pentagon because I'm part of anonymous!

That's not a question. Also, Phobos's very design funnels the amount of data sent down to less than what's sent in, meaning we do the opposite of an amplification attack.
